Hunza. Mountains, lakes, peaceful views, nature. Best time April to October. Hotels: Serena Hotel Hunza, Eagles Nest Hotel, Hunza Embassy Hotel, Darbar Hotel, Hunza View Hotel. Family friendly. Activities: Attabad Lake Boating, Baltit Fort, Rakaposhi View Point, Eagle's Nest Hike, Karimabad Bazaar.

Swat. Switzerland of Pakistan. Valleys, waterfalls, green meadows. Best time May to September. Hotels: Pearl Continental, Swat View Hotel, Swat Serena, Green Valley Hotel, White Palace. Activities: Malam Jabba Ski Resort, White Palace Museum, Kalam Valley, Mahodand Lake, Madyan Bazaar.

Murree. Family trips, snowfall, close to Islamabad. Best time December to February. Hotels: PC Bhurban, Hotel One Murree, Residency Hotel, Pine Hills Hotel, Murree Greens. Activities: Mall Road, Patriata Chairlift, Pindi Point, Kashmir Point, Bhurban Golf.

Nathia Gali. Hiking trails, cool weather, peaceful hill station. Best time June to August. Hotels: Green Retreat, Pine Top Hotel, Galiat Inn, Hill View Lodge. Activities: Mukshpuri Top Hike, Thandiani Road, Dunga Gali Pipeline Track, Nathia Gali Bazaar.

Shogran. Siri Paye meadows, short trips. Best time May to September. Hotels: Pine Park Hotel, Siri Paye Resort, Green Meadows Hotel, Shogran Heights. Activities: Siri Paye Meadows, Makra Peak Trek, Horse Riding, Photography.

Abbottabad. Pleasant weather, greenery, gateway to northern areas. Best time March to October. Hotels: Hotel One Abbottabad, Serena Hotel Abbottabad, Green Valley Resort, Pine View Hotel. Activities: Ilyasi Mosque, Shimla Peak, Thandiani Trip, Fawara Chowk.

Naran. Gateway to Saif ul Malook, lush green valleys. Best time May to September. Hotels: PTDC Motel Naran, Green Palace Hotel, River View Hotel, Mountain Lodge Naran. Activities: Saif ul Malook Lake, Lulusar Lake, Babusar Pass, Jeep Safari.

Skardu. K2 views, adventure hub. Best time April to October. Hotels: Shangrila Resort, K2 Motel, Baltistan Lodge, Mountain View Hotel. Activities: Kachura Lakes, Shangrila Resort, Deosai Plains, Shigar Fort.
